World Happiness Index: Data Visualization

This data visualization piece was inpsired by the World Happiness Index. The color labelling in the piece tells a story, where in green represents the top 5 countries who have to a large extent figured out happiness and red represents the last 20 countries who are yet to figure happiness out.

How is happiness calculated? The rankings are based on answers to the main life evaluation question asked in the poll. Through a tool called the Cantril ladder, respondents are asked to rate their own current lives on a scale from 0 to 10. Read more in the World Happiness Report FAQ.

Note: If you press your mouse, the countries associated with each number will be revealed.
